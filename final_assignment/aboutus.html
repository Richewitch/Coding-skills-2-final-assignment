About us

What is A Stitch in Time?

We are a Paris based clothing rental company. A Stitch in Time was founded in 2010 and starting in 2020 we are franchising our brand over all of France.

A Stitch in Time is a brand of body inclusive clothes rental which offers stylish clothing pieces for fashionable working and middle class women because we want to offer diverse timeless fashion, at a reasonable price, that women can feel good in without having to commit to buying them. We don't just rent clothes, we think about fashion, art, aesthetics and sustainability!

What is our mission?

We strive to help women of all shapes and sizes feel confident and empowered no matter where they are in their life, economically or physically, by offering them the sustainable practice of renting stylish high-quality pieces of clothing.

Our core values:
